Mines Game Rules

Objective

Your goal is to navigate the 25-tile grid by either clicking on a tile or choosing a random one. If you reveal a gem, your multiplier will increase as you progress to the next round. Reveal a mine, however, and you will lose the round and your bet.

Game Setup

  • The game is played on a 5x5 grid.
  • At the start, Diamonds and Mines are randomly placed under the tiles.

How to Play

  • Set your bet amount
  • Select the number of mines
  • Click the “Bet” button
  • Click on a tile
  • If it reveals a diamond, your multiplier will increase
  • If it reveals a mine, the game will end and you will lose your bet
  • You can either choose to cash out or keep playing until you land on a mine or discover all of the diamonds

Game Over

The game is over when the user chooses a tile with the mine in it.
